our story LOVE FOR FOOD

Boteco is a Brazilian bar and restaurant owned by Pedro and Ana. They started with a small kiosk, selling drinks and snacks like caipirinha and pastel. They opened Boteco, meaning “pub” or “tavern” in Portuguese, to offer a casual and lively place where people can enjoy Brazilian food and culture. Boteco is a fun, welcoming, and authentic place where people can relax and have a good time.
